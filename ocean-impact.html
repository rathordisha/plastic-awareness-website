Material Recovery Facility (MRF)

What is MRF?

A Material Recovery Facility (MRF) is a specialized plant that receives, separates, and prepares recyclable materials for marketing to end-user manufacturers.

MRF Process Flow:

  1. Collection: Mixed recyclables are collected and transported to MRF
  2. Pre-sorting: Manual removal of non-recyclable items
  3. Mechanical Separation:
    • Trommel screens for size separation
    • Magnetic separators for metals
    • Air classification for light materials
  4. Material Specific Processing:
    • Plastic sorting by resin type
    • Glass color separation
    • Paper grade sorting
  5. Baling: Materials compressed for transport
